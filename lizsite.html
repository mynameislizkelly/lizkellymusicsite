THE BETTER HALF

The better half is a collaboration of friends and other artists that is ever increasing. This collaboration consists of co-writing songs with her "better-half" of ten years, and musicians adding their expertise to recordings, producing/mixing/mastering, and playing live.

The Better Half includes Joni Russell-Kelly Julia Read, Anna Dart, Chris Crawford, and more.

KIDS MUSIC

Liz has been playing for kids for over 7 years. She played drums and sang back up for John Carlin and the Kids Music Underground but now currently performs solo playing various instruments and rocking out as hard as she can. She plays private parties and can be found playing at different public places such as the park. Stay updated to find out where Liz will be playing next. Check out her live section.
